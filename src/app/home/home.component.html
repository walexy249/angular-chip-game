Rules of Chip game
  1. Find the hidden Chip behind the images below. Out of 10 chances; 3 or more correct picks wins. 1 point for every correct pick.
  2. You need to hold a minimum of 500 $CHIP in other to play and earn
  3. If you hold between 500 - 2000 $CHIP, you have a maximum trial of 20 games per day
  4. If you hold between 2001 - 5000 (Basic CHIP), you have a maximum trial of 50 games per day
  5. if you hold between 5001 - 10,000 (Advanced CHIP) you have a maximum trial of 100 games per day
  6. VIP CHIP holders (10001 and above) has a maximum trial of 200 games per day
  7. You get 0.02 ETH for each game you win
